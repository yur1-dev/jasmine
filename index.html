About me

I'm Jasmine Javate Santos—living in Santa Cruz, Zaragoza, Nueva Ecije, and at 18 years old (born June 27, 2006), I thrive on reading, watching, eating, drawing, gaming, shopping, exploring trends, and solving puzzles, while my interests span music and singing, makeup and beauty, cultural exploration, and enjoying medical and law dramas.

I was a senior in high school with an intense passion in computers and technology before I began my IT career in college. I studied ICT, where I gained an understanding of the fundamentals of digital applications, computer systems, and programming. I liked working on computer issues and taking part in tech-related activities throughout high school. As I experimented with various technologies, my enthusiasm for IT increased, which inspired me to seek an information technology degree.
